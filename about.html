About Me

Hello! My name is Michael Wilcox, I was born an raised in Washington State, USA, and I love technology, fish keeping, and the great outdoors!

I consider myself to be a pretty open minded person who is willing to learn new things on a daily basis as well as try to teach someone something new whenever the opportunity arises. As such, I have lots of various hobbies that keep me pretty busy when I am not at work or working around my 5 acre property. My hobbies include, but are most definitely not limited to, fish keeping, chainsaw carving, digital photography and pc gaming (because console gaming just isn't as challenging).

Fish Keeping

Tropical fish have always fascinated me, but the real fun for me is in the aquascaping. In my opinion, nothing brings an aquarium to life more than a fully functional environment for the fish to live in. Live plants, real woods and rocks mixed with a community of tropical fish makes for a tank that is both peaceful to listen to and fun to stare at for hours! Not to mention, the possiblities of arrangments are endless.

Chainsaw Carving

I began chainsaw carving in mid 2019 out of shear boredom. I had just finished cutting up firewood for the day and decided to take my chainsaw and carve on a stump that was about 14 inches tall. I made my first mushroom! Since then, I have made benches, signs, bears, sea creatures, and much more!
